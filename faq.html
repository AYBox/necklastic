Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Is this elastic flat like floss?
A: No, it's round like regular string, and it is rubbery that if you stretch it, it will not rip.

Q: Can this make necklines smaller, or just prevent stretching?
A: Yes, it is used extensively to make necklines smaller & is a primary purpose of the product.

Q: If I want to make a neckline a bit smaller, will there be wrinkles?
A: It will not bunch (i.e. wrinkle) if you follow the instructions. The key is to make the 2 stitches, as per instructions. This allows the neckline to be pulled in mostly from the back so that the front is very smooth looking.
